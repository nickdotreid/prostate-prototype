Facts and Numbers

Over the course of your lifetime:

Risk of developing prostate cancer is 160 in 1,000

Risk of dying from prostate cancer is 30 in 1,000

Before PSA testing, a man's chances of:

Getting diagnosed with prostate cancer at some point: 90 in 1,000

Dying from prostate cancer: 30 in 1,000

Nowadays, with PSA testing, a man's chances of:

Getting diagnosed with prostate cancer at some point: 170 in 1,000

Dying from prostate cancer: 27 in 1,000
